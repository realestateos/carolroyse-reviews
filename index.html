Not Sure What to Say? Here Are Some Ideas

You don't have to write a lot. Two or three sentences is plenty.

  • What you were going through when we helped you (selling the family home, downsizing, buying your first house, etc.)
  • Who on the team you worked with
  • One thing that stood out about working with us
  • Whether you'd recommend us to friends or family

"Carol helped us sell our home of 30 years when we were ready to downsize. She made a stressful process feel easy and got us more than we expected. I already recommended her to my sister!"
